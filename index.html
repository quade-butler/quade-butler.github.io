Featured Paper

Gaussian Filtering Using a Spherical-Radial Double Exponential Cubature

Q. Butler, Y. Ziada, and S. A. Gadsden, IEEE Open Journal of Signal Processing, 2025.

This paper introduces the Double Exponential Cubature Kalman Filter (DECKF), a nonlinear Gaussian state estimator that uses a spherical-radial double exponential cubature rule to approximate Gaussian-weighted integrals in Bayesian filtering.

  • Derived the DE cubature construction, cubature points and weights, and full DECKF prediction/update recursion.
  • Included a stochastic stability analysis under stated assumptions on the nonlinear system and noise model.
  • Validated the method through numerical integration studies and nonlinear target tracking examples.
  • Benchmarked estimator accuracy and consistency against UKF, CKF, and Gauss-Hermite filtering baselines.
